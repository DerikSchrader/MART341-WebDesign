Derik Schrader

Derik Schrader is a student of the University of Montana Media Arts program, but has taken a strong interest in photography. Deriks interest in photography started during his military career while serving in Afghanistan and Iraq, and continues to grow today.

Derik uses a variety of camera types to capture images such as: a full frame mirrorless camera, drone, and smartphone to capture stunning and often moody images.
