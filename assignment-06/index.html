A Beginner's Guide To Photography Equipment

Equipment Essentials

  • A full-frame camera (kits are a good starting point)
  • A quality tripod (best you can afford)
  • Camera cleaning kit
  • A decent computer with a minimum of 16RAM
  • A decent monitor (higher resolution is best)
  • Editing software of choice
  • Photography book or class

Optional Equipment To Think About

  • Additional lenses beyond kit lenses
  • Camera flash
  • Strobe light (to freeze motion)
  • Diffuser (used to soften light)
  • Reflector
  • Tethering equipment (for laptop/desktop)

Getting started with photography can be very overwhelming and the cost can rise as quickly. Buying used equipment from reputable re-sellers is a good way to knock the price down versus buying new. The bottom line is get what you can afford and have fun!
